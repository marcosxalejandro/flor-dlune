## Core Concept

Power Rangers run to home or Football HQ to change ("morph") into their Flor D'Lune Sports football kits (tenue). During the morphing transformation they dance to the official Speed World Cup song (IShowSpeed - World Cup (Champions)).

- Each video features a **different popular viral meme dance**.
- The dance must feel **natural** within the scene (hallway, street, HQ entrance, dark chamber).
- **Elevated meme energy**: premium, cool, confident, athletic, funny without being childish or cheap. Strong body language, swag, high-fashion sports film quality.
- **Flor D'Lune aesthetic**: cosmic + mysterious + premium + playful viral. Deep black, cosmic purple, **Blossom pink (#E8A0BF)**, gold accents.
- **Power Rangers**: hyper-glossy realistic (helmets on for iconic silhouette + strong body language), not toy-like. Suits have cosmic details.
- **Football Kit (Flor D'Lune Sports)**: sleek black base jersey with cosmic purple gradient side panels, Blossom pink #E8A0BF shoulders/sleeves/cuffs, luxurious gold embroidered Flor D'Lune crest + subtle football/Speed detailing, matching shorts with gold trim, high socks, modern boots.

**Technical Requirements (all videos):**
- Aspect ratio: **9:16** (vertical, Reels / Stories / mobile-first / TikTok / YouTube Shorts ready)
- Duration: **8-12 seconds** (generated at 10s)
- High cinematic quality + **subtle film grain**
- Use the uploaded reference video as primary visual style reference (color grading, light, texture, grain, rhythm)
- No legible on-screen text (branding only via kit embroidery)

## Style Guide (locked)

**Colors:**
- Dominant: Deep inky black (#0A0A0F / near black)
- Secondary atmosphere: Cosmic purple
- Signature accent: Blossom pink (#E8A0BF)
- Highlights: Metallic gold + warm reflections

**Lighting:** High contrast cinematic, strong rims, volumetric rays when appropriate, never over-lit. Night scenes preferred for mystery.

**Grain & Post:** Subtle organic film grain (match reference), rich textures (matte + glossy on suits/kit), anamorphic lens characteristics (gentle flares, bokeh, breathing).

**Performance Direction:** 
- Athletic football player bodies + posture
- Power Ranger helmets stay on for most shots (iconic + mysterious)
- Dances feel owned and cool — elevated meme, not literal TikTok recreation
- Morphing = stylish, powerful, almost ritualistic clothing change that reads as transformation

**Music Note:** All motion and cuts designed to lock to IShowSpeed World Cup (Champions) beat structure. In final delivery, overlay the official track (or clean stems) in post. The generated videos are picture-locked to the imagined rhythm.
